Welcome!

I am a PhD student in Sociology at Charles University and a Researcher in Computational social science at LMU Munich. Previously I was a researcher at the Institute of Sociology of the Czech Academy of Sciences.

My thesis examines homophily in online dating, using digital trace data from online dating sites. Apart from that, I have co-authored papers on the division of household labor and partner preferences.

I am interested in computational social science (scraping, text as data, online experiments, new data).

I like dogs, baking, knitting (both in and outside of RMarkdown) and learning new things in R.
